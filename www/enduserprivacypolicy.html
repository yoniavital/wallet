11 Use of Cookies

Mobilechain Solution may direct you to mobile browsing pages which uses cookies, which may be operated by us or third parties (“Mobile-sites”).

Cookies (which may be HTML files, Flash files or other technology) are text files that help store user preferences and activity. “Web beacons” (also known as image tags, gif or web bugs) are small pieces of code used to collect data, such as counting page views or promotion views.

Mobile-sites may use “cookies”, “web beacons” and other similar information-gathering technologies (collectively, “Cookies“) to collect non-PII automatically as you access and browse the Mobile-site. This Policy covers the use of Cookies by Mobilechain only and does not cover the use of Cookies by any other third party.

Mobilechain may use both session ID Cookies and persistent Cookies, for the purpose of making it easier for you to navigate the Mobile-site. A session ID Cookie expires when you close your browser; a persistent Cookie remains for an extended period of time. You may remove persistent Cookies by following directions provided in your mobile browser’s “help” file. We set a persistent Cookie for such uses as to store your app name and/or user name so you don’t have to enter it more than once. Persistent Cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the users’ experience on the Mobile-site. If you reject Cookies, you may still use the Mobile-site.

By using Mobilechain Solution, whether as a registered user or otherwise, you acknowledge, understand and hereby agree that you are giving us your consent to gather information about your use of Mobilechain Solution through Cookies, when applicable.
